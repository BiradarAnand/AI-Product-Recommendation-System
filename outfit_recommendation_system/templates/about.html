About the Project

Understanding the purpose and technologies behind our system.

Project Objective

The Smart Outfit Recommendation System is designed to alleviate the daily decision fatigue of choosing what to wear. By intelligently analyzing user preferences regarding occasion, weather, gender, and personal style, the engine curates a complete, fashionable outfit in seconds.

AI Features

  • K-Nearest Neighbors Algorithm for content-based matching.
  • Decision Tree integration for predicting stylistic popularity.
  • Robust categorical feature encoding scaling across thousands of dataset items.
